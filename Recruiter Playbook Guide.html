What is the Playbook?
📚 Your end-to-end hiring toolkit for Tech & Engineering roles
  • A single-page tool that guides you from job profile through to offer — structured around SAP's Skills & AI hiring framework.
  • Everything auto-saves in your browser — close the tab and your work will be there when you return.
  • Contains a Recruiter view (default) and an HM View you can use on-screen during the RSM conversation.
The 6 Tabs — What Each One Does
1
Workflow Recruiter
Your end-to-end hiring map — 4 steps from Job Profile to Skill Evaluation. Use this as your process anchor at the start of every new requisition.
2
Strategy Meeting Recruiter
Structured RSM form with a directional questionnaire (Q1–11), Skills & Interviewer Allocation, Next Steps, and a free-text Summary box. Export your notes or send a Post-Strategy Email directly from here. Supports BrightHire mode.
3
Skills Prioritization Recruiter HM View
Embedded tool for agreeing the 5 Must-Have tech skills with your HM. Walk the HM through this on-screen during the RSM. Use the Send Email button to share the agreed skills summary.
4
Interview Guide Recruiter
7 standardised interview questions for Tech & Engineering roles, each with "What to Listen For" guidance. Export or email directly to the interview panel.
5
Job Descriptions Recruiter
Pre-built JD templates and guidance for SAP E&T roles. Use to sense-check or improve a JD before posting.
6
FAQ Recruiter HM View
Answers to common recruiter questions on skills, AI, and the hiring process. A separate HM-specific FAQ is visible when HM View is active.
How to Run a Strategy Meeting with the Playbook
1
Before the call — Open the Strategy Meeting tab. Fill in HM name, REQ ID, and date. Review Q1–11 to pick the questions most relevant to this role.
2
During the call — Type notes directly into the questionnaire fields. Switch to HM View and open Skills Prioritization to agree the 5 Must-Have skills on-screen with the HM.
3
Skills agreement — Use the Skills Prioritization tab to walk the HM through Must Have / Nice to Have / Not Required. Fill in the Skills & Interviewer Allocation section.
4
After the call — Complete Next Steps checkboxes. Add your Recruiter Summary. Click Send Email to copy the formatted summary and open Outlook — paste with Ctrl+V.
5
Export for records — Click Export & Download RSM Notes to save a full HTML copy of your notes for file-keeping or sharing with a covering recruiter.
Key Features at a Glance
👥
HM View
Shows a simplified view for walking the HM through skills on-screen. Never share the playbook link with the HM — this is a recruiter demo tool only.
🎤
BrightHire Mode
Toggle on if you recorded the RSM via BrightHire. Paste the AI summary into the BrightHire box — it replaces the questionnaire in the Post-Strategy Email.
💾
Auto-Save & Sessions
All fields save automatically in your browser. Use Save Session to store named drafts for different HMs or requisitions and reload them anytime.
📩
Send Email
One click copies a fully formatted email to your clipboard and opens Outlook. Press Ctrl+V in the Outlook compose window to paste — tables and colours included.
📝
Strategy Summary Box
For notes captured outside the tool (e.g. informal call or Teams chat). Fill this instead of — or in addition to — the questionnaire. Included in your export.
📄
Export RSM Notes
Downloads a complete HTML record of your RSM — questionnaire, skills, next steps, and summary. Use for file-keeping or briefing a covering recruiter.
Dos & Don'ts
✓ Do
Use HM View on your screen during the RSM — walk the HM through skills live.

Toggle BrightHire mode if you recorded the meeting — paste the AI summary instead of typing notes.

Save a named session for each requisition so you can switch between HMs without losing work.

Use the directional questionnaire as a guide — pick the questions relevant to the conversation, not all 11.
✗ Don't
Share the playbook link with the Hiring Manager — it is an internal recruiter tool.

Send the email without pasting into Outlook — click Send Email, then Ctrl+V in the compose window.

Skip the Skills & Interviewer Allocation section — this feeds the interview panel briefing.

Rely on browser history for saving — use Save Session for anything you need to retrieve later.
