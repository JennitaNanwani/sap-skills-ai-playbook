Key Features

Show skills to the HM on-screen. Never share the link — recruiter tool only.
🎤 BrightHire

💾 Auto-Save
All fields save in your browser. Use Save Session for multiple HMs.
📩 Send Email
Copies formatted email. Open Outlook and press Ctrl+V to paste.
How to Run a Strategy Meeting — Step by Step
1
Before the call — Open the Strategy Meeting tab. Enter HM name, REQ ID and date. Scan Q1–11 and pick the questions most relevant to this role and HM.
2
During the call — Type your notes directly into the questionnaire fields. Use the Strategy Summary box if you prefer free-form notes over the structured questions.
3
Skills conversation — Switch to HM View and open the Skills Prioritization tab. Walk the HM through Must Have / Nice to Have / Not Required on your screen. Agree the top 5 skills.
4
Skills & interviewers — Return to Strategy Meeting. Complete the Skills Defined Summary section — enter the 5 agreed skills and assign an interviewer to each.
5
Next Steps — Tick the relevant Recruiter and HM action checkboxes. Add any additional notes or HM commitments in the free-text fields.
6
Send & export — Click Send Email to copy the formatted summary and open Outlook — paste with Ctrl+V. Click Export to download a full HTML record for your files.
Using BrightHire Mode
1
Toggle BrightHire mode on before or after the call.
2
Paste the BrightHire AI summary into the BrightHire Summary box — this replaces the questionnaire in the Post-Strategy Email.
3
The Strategy Meeting Summary box is hidden in BrightHire mode. Still complete Skills Allocation and Next Steps as normal.
Dos & Don'ts
✓ Do
Use HM View on your screen during the RSM to walk the HM through skills live.
Save a named session per requisition so you can switch between HMs without losing work.
Use the questionnaire as a guide — pick relevant questions, not all 11.
Complete Skills Allocation and assign interviewers before sending the summary email.
✗ Don't
Share the playbook link with the Hiring Manager — internal recruiter tool only.
Forget to paste in Outlook after clicking Send Email — the email is in your clipboard.
Rely on browser history — use Save Session for anything you need to retrieve later.
Skip Skills Allocation — it feeds the interview panel briefing and export.
