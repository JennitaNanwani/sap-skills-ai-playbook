Run a Strategy Meeting — 5 Steps
1
Prepare — before the callOpen Strategy Meeting tab · fill HM name, REQ ID & date · scan Q1–11 and pick what's relevant
2
Capture — during the callType notes into the questionnaire · or use the free-text Summary box for your own notes
3
Skills — agree with HM on-screenSwitch to HM View → Skills Prioritization · walk HM through Must Have / Nice to Have · agree top 5
4
Assign — interviewers & next stepsFill Skills & Interviewer Allocation · tick Next Steps checkboxes · add HM commitments
5
Send — email & exportClick Send Email → paste in Outlook (Ctrl+V) · click Export to download your full notes

Dos & Don'ts
✓  Do
Walk HM through skills live in HM View
Save a session per REQ — keeps HMs separate
Pick the most relevant questions, not all 11
Assign interviewers before sending summary
✗  Don't
Share the playbook link with the HM
Forget to paste in Outlook after Send Email
Skip Skills Allocation — feeds panel brief
Rely on browser history — use Save Session
💡  Quick Reminders
BrightHire on → BrightHire box shows
BrightHire off → Strategy Summary shows
HM View → your screen only — do not share
Send Email → must paste in Outlook (Ctrl+V)
