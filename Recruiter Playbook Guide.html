⚡  Run a Strategy Meeting in 5 Steps
1
PrepareOpen Strategy Meeting tab — fill in HM, REQ ID & date
2
Capture notesType into questionnaire fields or use the free-text Summary box
3
Skills agreementSwitch to HM View → Skills Prioritization → agree top 5 on-screen
4
Assign interviewersBack in Strategy Meeting → fill Skills & Interviewer Allocation
5
Send & exportClick Send Email → paste in Outlook (Ctrl+V) → Export for your records

⚡  Dos & Don'ts
✓ Do
Use HM View on your screen — walk skills live with the HM
Save a named session per REQ to switch between HMs
Pick relevant questions — you don't need all 11
Assign interviewers before sending the summary
✗ Don't
Share the playbook link with the HM
Forget to paste in Outlook after Send Email
Skip Skills Allocation — it feeds the panel brief
Rely on browser history — use Save Session
💡 Remember
BrightHire on? → BrightHire box shows.
BrightHire off? → Summary box shows.
HM View? → On your screen only.
